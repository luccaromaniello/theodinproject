Belle's Hamburguer Buns

Description

Recipe of Hamburguer Buns (actually from Belle's mom, as mentioned in the original website), fluflly and delicious for a weekend afternoon!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 5 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 (.25 ounce) envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon water
